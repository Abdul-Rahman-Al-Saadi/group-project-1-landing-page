Mazes Await! Can You Find the Exit?

Dungeon Crawler is a 2D maze game where you escape a dangerous dungeon

You play as siblings Mason and Lily Brooks, wrongly exiled for stealing a relic. Trapped in a dangerous dungeon, they must navigate its perils to clear their names, uncover a dark conspiracy, and retrieve the relic to escape.

Characters

Mason Brooks

Role: The Protector

Mason, the younger sibling, is brave and strategic. Armed with a sword, he confronts the dungeon's dangers, determined to clear their names despite the risks.

Role: The Strategist

Lily, the older sister, is quick-witted and resourceful. Using her agility and problem-solving skills, she navigates the dungeon, driven by curiosity to uncover the truth behind their exile.
